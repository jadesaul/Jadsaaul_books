The Baltic Sanction

An explosive spy thriller. Non-official CIA officer Lex Jackson, assigned to the CIA station in Copenhagen, is dispatched to uncover who is behind the White Nights, a shadow organization working to undermine the U.S. president. When his investigation reveals a mole inside the CIA, Jackson works to stay ahead of an assassin hired to kill him.

Former KGB black operator Roald Popov uncovers intel that the Foreign Intelligence Service of the Russian Federation has sleeper cells to infiltrate the echelons of the U.S. and the Canadian governments. When his cover is blown, Popov races to get the intel into the correct hands, protect his daughter, and escape Russia alive.
